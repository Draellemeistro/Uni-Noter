> “*Risk Management is defined as the coordinated activities to direct and control an enterprise with regard to risk. Risk can be viewed as a challenge to achieving objectives and risk management as the activity undertaken to predict challenges and lower their chances of occurring and their impact*”.

# Cyber risk

> [!NOTE] What is Cyber Risk?
> “*Information security risk is the potential that a given threat will exploit vulnerabilities of an asset or group of assets and thereby cause harm to the organization*”. 
>  -- **ISO 270005:2011**

# Elements of risk
- **Impact**
- **threat**
- **Vulnerability**
- **Consequence**
- **Likelihood**

# Categories/Taxonomies of risk
- **Enteprise Risk**
	- Operational risk
	- Financial risk
	- Compliance risk
- **Technology risk**
![[Pasted image 20240608212630.png]]

# Risk Management life cycle
1. **Risk Identification**
2. **Risk Assessment**
3. **Risk Response and Mitigation**
4. **Risk Monitoring and Reporting** (then back to 1.)
![[Pasted image 20240608212749.png]]

# Types of risk assessments
- **Qualitative risk assessment**
- **Quantitative risk assessment**
- **A mix of both**
